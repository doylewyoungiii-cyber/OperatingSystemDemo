DAILY — 10 minutes
  1. Cadence: work the red rows top-down. Call or email, then hit Touched today.
  2. Cold Calls: clear any follow-up dated today or earlier (shown red).
  3. Log anything new the moment it happens — opps, spec activity, CEU events.
WEEKLY — 30 minutes, Monday
  1. Sales Import Center: upload Monday's bookings report; confirm the matches.
  2. Agencies: scan health scores; open the 3 lowest and update priority + blocker.
  3. Opportunities: advance stages; every open opp needs a real next step.
  4. Spec Pipeline: confirm each spec moved or has a dated action.
  5. AIA CEU: close open follow-ups; nudge presenters mid-pipeline.
  6. LinkedIn: draft this week's two posts.
MONTHLY — 1 hour, first Friday
  1. Overview: read the variance matrix; note months slipping vs goal.
  2. Training: assign next courses; chase overdue and failed quizzes.
  3. Reports: export the Agency Scorecard CSV for the leadership readout.
  4. Agencies: re-tier / re-categorize anyone whose trajectory changed.
  5. Calendar: confirm next month's trips against Fix/Build priorities.
Definitions
Health ScoreWeighted 0–100 composite: Revenue pacing, Opportunities, Training, CEU/AIA, Strategic accounts, Reporting compliance. Weights live in Settings; the per-agency breakdown is on each agency page. Revenue pacingYTD 2026 vs the same months of 2025. New agencies with revenue score 80; new with none score 35. Reporting complianceDays since last logged touch: ≤14d = 100 · ≤30d = 70 · ≤60d = 40 · beyond = 10. CategoryProtect = keep close, don't break it · Build = invest to grow · Push = demand more from existing capacity · Fix = behavioral or structural problem · Exit = transition out. Spec stagesTarget Identified → Specifier Engaged → Basis of Design → Named Specification → Bid Stage → Awarded. A spec is only "Named" when XtraLight appears on the drawing. Bronze / Silver / GoldBronze = all Level 1 courses passed (quiz ≥ pass mark). Silver = Bronze + a certified specialist track or an approved AIA presenter. Gold = Bronze + both. Cadence flagsGreen ≤14 days since touch · Yellow 15–30 · Red >30 or never — red rows are the day's call list.
Data Hygiene Rules
  • Notes format: Date – Who – What – Next. No note without a next.
  • Every open opportunity has an owner and a dated next step.
  • Value "TBD" is allowed for 30 days, then estimate or kill it.
  • CEU events get follow-up within 5 business days — no exceptions.
  • Status dots are yours: Green = on plan, Yellow = watch, Red = act this week.
Good to Know
  • Every Monday: upload the ERP "Order Bookings Summary by Sales Person" in the Sales Import Center: choose file → check matches → confirm. Each upload becomes a snapshot; roll back any upload from Upload History.
  • Everything saves automatically to this browser as you type.
  • Click any agency name anywhere to jump to its full page.
  • All six reports export exactly what's on screen as CSV.
  • Dropdown choices, courses, weights and pass marks are editable in Settings.
  • Reset (in Settings) restores the shipped dataset if things get messy.
